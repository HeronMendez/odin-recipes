Pizza

Description

Homemade pepperoni pizza is all about creating a flavorful and satisfying dish with a crispy crust that's loaded with tangy tomato sauce, melted cheese, and spicy pepperoni. This recipe allows you to enjoy the taste of a pizzeria in the comfort of your own home.

Ingredients

For the Pizza Dough

  • 1 pound (about 3 1/4 cups) all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

For the Pizza Toppings

  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce or marinara sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni (adjust to your preference)
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano (optional)
  • Crushed red pepper flakes (optional, for extra heat)

Steps

Making the Pizza Dough

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and active dry yeast. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. Add the olive oil and salt to the yeast mixture and stir to combine.
  3. Gradually add the flour, mixing until the dough comes together.
  4. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead it for about 5 minutes until it's smooth and elastic. If the dough is too sticky, you can add a bit more flour.
  5. Place the dough in a lightly greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise for 1-2 hours or until it has doubled in size.

Assembling the Pizza

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) or as high as your oven will go. If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while it's preheating.
  2. Punch down the risen dough and divide it into two equal portions to make two 12-inch pizzas.
  3. Roll out one portion of the dough on a floured surface to your desired pizza crust thickness.
  4. If using a pizza stone, carefully transfer the rolled-out dough onto a piece of parchment paper. If not, you can assemble the pizza on a greased baking sheet.
  5. Spread half of the pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  6. Sprinkle half of the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  7. Arrange the pepperoni slices on top of the cheese.
  8. If desired, sprinkle with dried oregano and crushed red pepper flakes for added flavor and heat.

Baking the Pizza

  1. Carefully transfer the pizza, along with the parchment paper (if using), onto the preheated pizza stone or place the baking sheet with the pizza in the oven.
  2. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown, and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  3. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a minute or two before slicing.
  4. Repeat the process to make the second pizza with the remaining dough and toppings.

Slice your homemade pepperoni pizza, and enjoy the mouthwatering combination of crispy crust, rich tomato sauce, gooey cheese, and spicy pepperoni. It's a satisfying and comforting meal for pizza lovers of all ages.
