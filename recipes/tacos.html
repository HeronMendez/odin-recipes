Tacos

Description

Carne asada tacos feature thinly sliced, marinated, and grilled steak served on warm corn tortillas. The tender, well-seasoned beef is typically accompanied by fresh toppings and salsa, creating a burst of flavor in every bite.

Ingredients

For the Carne Asada Marinade

  • 1 1/2 pounds flank steak or skirt steak
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh orange juice
  • 1/4 cup fresh lime juice
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For Assembling the Tacos

  • Corn tortillas (small street taco size)
  • Sliced avocado or guacamole
  • Chopped fresh cilantro
  • Diced onion
  • Salsa (salsa verde, pico de gallo, or your choice)
  • Lime wedges for garnish

Steps

Marinating the Carne Asada

  1. In a bowl, combine the minced garlic, fresh orange juice, fresh lime juice, fresh lemon juice, chopped cilantro, vegetable oil, ground cumin, chili powder, paprika, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to create the marinade.
  2. Place the flank steak or skirt steak in a large zip-top bag or a shallow dish. Pour the marinade over the meat, making sure it's well coated. Seal the bag or cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight for maximum flavor.

Grilling the Carne Asada

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. You can also use a stovetop grill pan.
  2. Remove the marinated steak from the refrigerator and let it sit at room temperature for about 30 minutes before grilling.
  3. Grill the steak for about 4-6 minutes per side, or until it reaches your preferred level of doneness. Flank steak is typically served medium-rare to medium for the best tenderness.
  4. Once the steak is done, transfer it to a cutting board, cover it loosely with foil, and let it rest for 5-10 minutes.
  5. After resting, thinly slice the carne asada against the grain.

Assembling the Tacos

  1. Heat the corn tortillas on the grill or in a dry skillet for a minute or two on each side until warm and pliable.
  2. To assemble each taco, place a few slices of carne asada on a warm tortilla.
  3. Top with sliced avocado or guacamole, chopped cilantro, diced onion, and your choice of salsa.
  4. Serve the carne asada tacos with lime wedges on the side for squeezing over the top.

Enjoy your homemade carne asada tacos, filled with tender, flavorful beef and fresh, zesty toppings. These tacos are perfect for a family dinner or a fun taco night with friends.
